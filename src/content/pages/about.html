About Us

East Brampton Baptist Church is an Independent Fundamental Baptist Church sharing the love of God in Brampton, Ontario. Please accept our personal invitation to come and visit with us soon. We look forward to meeting you!

Psalm 122:1

A Bible-believing church sharing the Gospel in Brampton since 2008

We're a friendly community of believers committed to glorifying God through worship, fellowship, and service.

At East Brampton Baptist Church, we believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice. Our mission is to preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, build up believers in their faith, and reach our community with the love of God. Whether you're a long-time believer or just exploring Christianity, you'll find a warm welcome here. We invite you to join us for Sunday services and experience the joy of worshiping together.

What We
Stand For

Rooted in the Gospel, our church life is guided by biblical truth that shapes how we worship, serve, and walk together in faith. These foundational beliefs inspire everything we do as a community.

What We Believe

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God, and that salvation comes through faith alone in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again.

How to Be Saved

Salvation is a free gift of God's grace. Acknowledge you are a sinner, believe Jesus Christ died for your sins and rose again, and receive Him as your personal Savior.
