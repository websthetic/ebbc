Planning a Visit?

Here are a few questions you might have:

1

When Do You Meet?

We gather every Sunday morning for worship services at 9:45am for Sunday School, 11am for the main service and 2pm for the Afternoon service. Small group meetings take place through the week in a variety of locations across the city. For more information, contact Pastor Lito.

2

What should I wear?

Dress modestly and comfortably. God looks at the heart (1 Samuel 16:7), not your clothing. You'll see a range of attire from casual to Sunday best - all are welcome!

3

What about my kids?

During our 11am service, we run a children’s Sunday school, Junior Kindergarten through to Grade 5. You are welcome to bring your children into the service with you. About 15-20 minutes into the service, kids will be dismissed to go to Sunday school.

4

What is expected of me?

You're welcome just as you are! Bring your questions, your concerns, or simply your curiosity about God. We won't put you on the spot or ask you to stand and introduce yourself. During the service, we take a moment to greet one another—feel free to join in or simply enjoy observing. The offering is for our members, so please don't feel any pressure to give as a guest. After the service, you're welcome to stay for fellowship or head out whenever you need to. Either way, we're glad you came!

5

Is East Brampton Baptist Church accessible?

East Brampton Baptist Church is an accessible facility with accessible parking, a wheelchair accessible entry and accessible restroom facilities on the main floor.

Visit Us

Our Location

We are located at 955 Clark Blvd, Brampton, ON L6T 5L3, at the intersection of Clark Boulevard and Bramalea Road.

Getting Here

By Car: There is ample free parking available for those travelling by car. Our parking lot entrance is accessible from Clark Boulevard.

By Transit: We are conveniently accessible by Brampton Transit. Take bus route 15 (Bramalea) or route 52 (Countryside), both stop near the intersection of Bramalea Road and Clark Boulevard, just a short walk from our church.

The building is fully accessible with wheelchair parking, ramps, and accessible washroom facilities.
